Taj Mahal - Agra

The Taj Mahal is a symbol of love, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. It is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Top Attractions:

  • Taj Mahal itself
  • Agra Fort
  • Fatehpur Sikri

Gateway of India - Mumbai

The Gateway of India is a monumental archway overlooking the Arabian Sea in Mumbai. It was built to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary to India in 1911.

Top Attractions:

  • The Gateway of India
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus
  • Marine Drive

Qutub Minar - Delhi

Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the tallest brick minaret in the world. It was built by Qutb-ud-din Aibak in the 12th century.

Top Attractions:

  • Qutub Minar
  • Iron Pillar of Delhi
  • Alai Minar
